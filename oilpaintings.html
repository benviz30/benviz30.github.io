Oil Paintings

"In Moldova, my career as an artist was centered on creating large oil Paintings. For a time, I stretched my own canvases, mixed my own paints, and even built and gilded my own frames. Although I've been working much more in watercolor lately, I welcome every opportunity to paint in oil. Although it is a more costly, lengthy, & laborious process, I find it incredibly exciting & fulfilling."
